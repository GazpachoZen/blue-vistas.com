About Danoggin

Danoggin is designed to support individuals with occasional cognitive challenges—especially those triggered by dehydration, fatigue, or stress. It delivers simple prompts throughout the day and alerts loved ones if responses are missed or concerning.

✓
Simple, non-intrusive questions delivered at customizable intervals to monitor cognitive wellness.
✓
Track check-in performance with immediate notifications when alerts are missed or incorrectly answered.
✓
User-friendly interface designed with strict privacy controls, letting you decide who sees what information.
