Privacy Policy

How we protect your data and respect your privacy

Effective Date: May 15, 2025

Blue Vista Solutions ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Danoggin mobile application ("App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please do not access or use our app.

At a Glance: Danoggin is a cognitive monitoring app that helps individuals track cognitive functions through regular check-ins. We collect only the data necessary to provide this service, we never sell your data, and we implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Contact Information: Email addresses of emergency contacts or designated observers you choose to add.
  • Device Information: Device ID, operating system, and mobile network information.

1.2 Health and Cognitive Data

We collect information related to your cognitive function, including:

  • Responses to cognitive check-in questions
  • Timestamps of when check-ins are completed or missed
  • Response time patterns
  • Performance metrics related to cognitive challenges
  • Any self-reported conditions or medications that you voluntarily provide

We understand this data is particularly sensitive, and we handle it with the utmost care as described throughout this policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core features and functionality
  • Process and deliver cognitive check-ins and assessments
  • Notify your designated observers when check-ins are missed or concerning
  • Identify patterns or changes in cognitive function over time
  • Improve and personalize your experience with the App
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Maintain the security and integrity of our App

3. How We Share Your Information

We prioritize your privacy and limit the sharing of your information to the following circumstances:

3.1 Sharing with Designated Observers

We will share your check-in status and performance metrics with individuals you've explicitly designated as observers. You can add, modify, or remove these observers at any time through the App settings.

3.2 Service Providers

We may share information with third-party vendors and service providers who need access to perform services on our behalf, such as:

  • Cloud storage providers
  • Analytics providers
  • Customer service and technical support providers

All service providers are bound by contractual obligations to keep your information confidential and use it only for the purposes for which we disclose it to them.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information where required by law or if we believe that such action is necessary to:

  • Comply with a legal obligation
  • Protect and defend our rights or property
  • Prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the App
  • Protect the personal safety of App users or the public

We never sell your data: We will never sell or rent your personal information or cognitive data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to secure your personal information from accidental loss and unauthorized access, use, alteration, and disclosure. All information you provide to us is stored on secure servers.

Your cognitive data and personal information are encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard encryption protocols to protect your data.

However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information and cognitive data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information and cognitive data within 30 days, except where we are required to maintain certain data to comply with our legal obligations.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

6.1 Access and Update

You can access and update your personal information directly through the App settings. You can also contact us to request access to, correction, or deletion of your personal information.

6.2 Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.

6.3 Notifications and Communications

You can manage notification preferences through the App settings. You may opt out of non-essential communications from us at any time.

6.4 Observers and Sharing

You have complete control over who can observe your cognitive check-in data. You can add, remove, or modify your designated observers at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 16 without parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. We will also provide a notification within the App or via email before the changes become effective.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
